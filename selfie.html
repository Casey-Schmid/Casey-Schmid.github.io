What is a p5.js Selfie

This is a special kind of self portrait that is not using a camera nor such materials as pencils or paint. Rather, this kind of selfie is made using the "Processing" and P5.js softwares. Using these softwares, artists can make self portraits that are simple yet abstract and still recognizable, resembling minimalist or pop art converted to didigtal to new media. They are many books and online guides on how to use these softwares. I made this self-portrait in April 2025, and I am proud of the final result
