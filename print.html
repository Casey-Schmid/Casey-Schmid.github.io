What is NetArt?

This is simply a couple of 3D printed version of my Minecraft Pixar ball sculpture, also made in March 2025. The first one is merely a rough draft, while the second is the official final draft. 3D printing seems like a complicated process, but once you have the right tools and softwares, including Flashforge, a 3D printing machine, and flash drive, you can make a great model.
